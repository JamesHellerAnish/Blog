Best Weekend getaways from Mumbai/Pune

Home Blog Best Weekend getaways from Mumbai/Pune

Are you going Pune/Mumbai for a business trip or for your education and worrying that the place has not much to offer us for a perfect weekend?

Then definitely you are not familiar with the hills where you can have calmness and peace kissing you or adventure sports where you can blow your mind off or beaches where you can reset your emotions and start afresh and what not.

Pune, the cultural capital of Maharashtra, epitomizing Marathi culture has emerged as the centre of modern architecture and hub of art, music and theatres.

The Queen of Deccan has much to offer that can relieve your emotions.

The charm of Mumbai never fails to attract travellers from not just within India but all over the world, yet all we city dwellers crave for a little change from our routine every now and then, to break the monotonous city life, and escape into a perfect weekend place to relax, feel peace, start afresh and charge up for days to come.

City, where Gateway of India is situated, where Elephanta caves spread their elegance, where the heart of Bollywood shows their legacy, has a lot more to offer for perfect weekends.

  1. Sinhagad

    Being in close vicinity of the city, it is one of the most favourite places for locals to spend a perfect weekend. It is an ancient fortress on a hill known for its Maratha architecture. Caves and carvings tell the stories of times 2000 years ago and give insights of Maratha era.

    Perfect for nature lovers. Trekking at hill-top viewing greenery all around and majestic beauty of hills—PERFECT!!!!

    Distance from Pune: 37 km

    Best Time to Visit: June-March

    Places to visit: Sinhagad Fort

  2. Kamshet

    The city of Kamshet offers a perfect weekend getaway from Pune. Being just 50 km away from the city, it is one of the favourite top choices of people living in the city. This picturesque hill station, hidden in an elegant village in the Western Ghats, is famous for paragliding. Paragliding here gives you the chance to view dainty villages, pretty waterfalls, greenery and beauty of nature while you are feeling the fresh air in…..should it is missed?? Obviously no!

    Distance from Pune: 48 km

    Best Time to Visit: October-June

    Places to visit: Bhairi hills and Kondeshwar Temple

  3. Pawna Lake

    Want to break the monotonous routine and getaway to enjoy the ideal weekend?? Pawna lake is the place. This huge lake near Lonavala is a must-visit with family and friends because of its splendid beauty as well as its strategic location. Make sure you stay at the banks of the lake to see the splendid sunrise and sunset amidst nature with beauty at its peak.

    Distance from Pune: 52 km

    Best Time to Visit: October-May

    Places to visit: Pawna Lake, Visapur Fort, Bhaja Caves, and Lion’s Point

  4. Bedse Caves

    Bedse Caves, built-in 60 BC, are arranged in Maval taluka, Pune, Maharastra. Illuminated by sunrise and enveloped in beauty, these rock-cut caves are the perfect weekend gateway for couples. These caves are built in a way that they are visible only from the top, so make sure you feel the fun and adventure in climbing 400 stairs. Once reached, don’t miss out to understand the regional history of Pune.

    Distance from Pune: 56 km

    Best Time to Visit: July to September

    Places to visit: The cave itself is a huge attraction.

  5. Lavasa

    One of the most accessible honeymoon places near Pune, it is widely known for its scenic waterfall. It is a secretly planned hill city, with a man-made lake that takes you instantly to Europe. You can also enjoy trekking, camping, zip-lining and rappelling in the area.

    Distance from Pune: 60 km

    Best Time to Visit: Monsoon months

    Places to visit: City itself has a lot to explore.

  1. Lohagadh

    One of the finest and gorgeous places to visit and is located at a height of 3600 feet. This place is the perfect fusion of natural beauty and wonderful architecture. Pleasant weather and fresh air spice up the beauty of this place and justify your choice of spending the weekend here.

    Distance from Pune: 65 km

    Best Time to Visit: Monsoon months

    Places to visit: Lohagad Fort, Tikona Fort and Tung Fort

  2. Lonavala

    Lonavala is the favourite of all couples and honeymooners and is located very close to Pune and Mumbai. Located in the Western Ghats, it has verdant greenery and scenic beauty. It has lush slopes, raging waterfalls, sparkling ponds and high viewpoints, making it a complete ‘not to be missed’ package. Other than love birds, this place is popular among trekkers and hikers. It has several caves and Buddhist shrines of historical relevance too.

    Distance from Pune: 66.5 km

    Best Time to Visit: Year-round

    Places to visit: Bhaja Caves, Bushi Dam, Karla Caves, Rajmachi Fort, Ryewood Lake

  3. Anarkali

    Famous for its white-sand beaches and crystal clear waters, Tarkarli is a good choice if you are interested in water-sports such as Banana Ride, Kayaking, Jet Ski, and scuba diving. It is also a protected reserve for marine life. Don’t miss out the malvani cuisine, it offers.

    Distance from Mumbai: 56 km

    Best Time to Visit: October to May

    Places to visit: Malvan, Tarkarli beach, Sindhudurg fort, Padma Garh fort, Dhampur lake, and Achra beach

  4. Dehna

    Love fairyland??? This is the pace for you. Located in a remote side village, this place is inhabited by tribals and have hidden streams of freshwater. This is the best place to charge up yourself for coming days by losing yourself to nature and feel the beauty.

    Distance from Mumbai: 112.5 km

    Best Time to Visit: June to September

    Places to visit: Fireflies festival is a must-attend.

  5. Diveagar

    A village in the Raigad district, Diveagar is famous for its serene beaches and quaint waters. It is a splendid 15 km stretch of the white sand spread between Harihareshwar and Shrivardhan. Want to take your lover for the perfect weekend and quality time alone?? This is the place for you!!

    Distance from Mumbai: 170 km

    Best Time to Visit: October to March

    Places to visit: Suvarna Ganesh Temple, Shrivardhan, Harihareshwar, Somaji Temple, Jiwaneshwar Temple, Bhagamandala beach, and Kuda Leni
