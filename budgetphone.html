Budget Smartphone Under Rs. 20000

Home Blog Budget Smartphone Under Rs. 20000

The year 2020 is turning out to be a year we all had never expected, but there is still one good thing happening in the year 2020 and that is the rise of mid-range smartphone all under one’s budget of 20K.

The best phone under Rs 20,000 offers almost all the features of a high-end smartphone which includes high refresh rate displays, high-resolution multi-camera setups that include ultra wide lenses, macro lenses and more, new designs, and long battery life, fast charging, great performance and premium design.

So are you looking to buy the best mobiles under Rs 20,000?

You can now choose your favourite from our curated list of top 9 mobiles (in no particular order) under Rs 20,000 in India.

  • Realme X2:

    The Realme X2 has a screen size of 6.4-inch with a Super AMOLED Full HD+ display and a 1080 x 2340 display resolution. It has small bezels, and a screen-to-body ratio of 91.9 per cent and has an aspect ratio of 19.5:9. It is provided with Corning Gorilla Glass 5 protection and also is equipped with an in-display fingerprint scanner. Realme X2 sports a 64MP + 8MP + 2MP + 2MP quad rear camera setup and has a 32MP selfie shooter. Moreover, it packs a 4000mAh battery that supports 30W fast charging. Realme X2 can rightly be judged as one of the best Realme mobile phones under the budget of Rs 20000.

  • Xiaomi Poco F1:

    The Xiaomi Poco F1 is the best value-for-money smartphone among the phones under 20000 available in the market because it is equipped with incredible performance chips and comes in an armoured variant. This phone is powered by the most powerful 10nm chip and the Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 processor at an extremely affordable price, which is mainly designed to handle heavy usage, be it gaming or work. The smartphone not only ensures quality performance but aso has good camera features. The 12+5MP rear camera when used captures incredible shots and the 20MP selfie camera gives a perfect click for you. The brand has also added an enhanced cooling technology in to the device which keeps both the CPU and GPU in a controlled environment, hence ensuring a better performance. It can easily handle multitasking because of its 8GB RAM and it stays working for the entire day because of its 4000mAh battery, that too on a single charge.

  • Vivo Z1 Pro:

    It has a unique punch-hole design which initially just for the premium segment smartphone. It also provides you with a massive 5,000mAh battery and a triple camera setup for clicking good pictures in all kinds of conditions. There is a AI powered 32MP selfie camera on the handset to ensure that you take beautiful selfies. With a full screen 6.5 inch display and a massive battery, you can rely on Vivo Z1 Pro to act as a perfect multimedia companion and this makes it stand out best phone under Rs. 20000

  • Samsung Galaxy M40:

    The Galaxy M40 is one of the best mobile phones to buy under 20000 as it features innovative technology and a beautiful design. It’s not AMOLED however it provides you with a bright TFT panel and thanks to its new display design, its screen stretches to the edges thus allowing one a fully immersive viewing experience. It has a 6.3-inch FHD+ display that has a screen-to-body ratio of 91.8%! It has the triple rear camera technology with 32MP + 8MP + 5MP which is another great addition to this model as this can be used to capture stunning 4K video and with the super slow-motion feature it adds an even greater creative effect. The phone is only 7.9mm in thickness, but this can be attributed to the fact that it has a strong 3500mAh battery. The back body of this phone is made with glossy plastic and has gradient finish.

  • Realme 6 Pro:

    The Realme 6 Pro defines the legacy of Realme's Pro series of incrementally adding new features in the mid-range segment mobile phones. It has 90Hz refresh rate display and a Snapdragon 720G SoC along with a 64MP quad camera setup. On the front you have a dual punch-hole camera, and a 4300mAh battery with 30W fast charging. Its software has also gone massive improvements. Which makes it a good wholesome package on offer for this budget.

  • Redmi Note 8 Pro:

    The Note 8 Pro is one of the fastest phone available well under Rs 20,000 because of itsMediaTek Helio G90T SoC. The phone has a 64MP primary camera, along with an 8MP ultra-wide sensor, a macro lens of 2MPand another depth sensor of 2MP. The Note 8 Pro is also equipped with in-built Alexa allowing you to go hands-free while controlling your home appliances.

  • Xiaomi POCO X2:

    Talking about best smartphones under 20K, POCO X2 can never be missed because of its 120Hz refresh rate display and a powerful processor. Talk about value for money and the POCO X2 offers a great camera performance which is proved by its 64MP primary sensor, an absolute impressive battery life, and a premium design. All these features make this handset an affordable one in our range.

  • Samsung Galaxy M31:

    The Samsung Galaxy M31 with a 6,000mAh battery inside is a perfect phone for those looking who are looking for a two day long battery life. It's powered by an efficient Exynos 9611 SoC and has a bright and compact AMOLED display with a 32MP front camera drilled into the display. It's also the first M-series smartphone to come with a 64MP quad camera setup with Samsung offering another 8MP ultrawide sensor, a 5MP macro sensor and another 5MP depth sensor.

  • Vivo S1:

    As you can see from our list Vivo has released some really good smartphone in the mid-range and the S1 falls right into this category. Vivo S1 comes with a capable 32MP selfie camera which in return makes it popular among the young generation. The phone offers a large display in a relatively compact frame because of the use of the waterdrop notch display design. It is also equipped with an in-display fingerprint sensor and a stylish design.
